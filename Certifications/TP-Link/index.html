    The TP-Link Certified Enterprise Switching and Routing Certification, formally known as TPNA ERS (TP-Link Network Associate – Enterprise Routing and Switching), is a foundational certification designed for IT professionals who want to master the basics of enterprise networking using TP-Link technologies, specifically routers and switches in standalone deployments. This certification validates your ability to plan, configure, deploy, and maintain enterprise-grade wired networks using TP-Link's product lineup. It is part of the TP-Link Network Associate (TPNA) certification track and is a gateway to more advanced TP-Link certifications.
